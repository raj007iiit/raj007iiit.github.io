Research Focus

Research Interests

Working at the intersection of responsible AI, explainability, and distributed learning systems.

🔍

Explainable AI (XAI)

Making ML models interpretable and transparent for high-stakes decisions using SHAP, LIME, Grad-CAM, Captum.

⚖️

Responsible & Fair AI

Ensuring fairness, accountability, and ethical deployment of AI systems across sensitive domains.

🔗

Federated Learning

Privacy-preserving distributed machine learning for IoMT, UAV, and edge systems.

🧠

LLMs & NLP

Efficiency, fine-tuning, bias analysis, and explainability of large language models.

👁️

Computer Vision

Deep learning for medical imaging, plant disease detection, and remote sensing classification.

🌐

Social Network Analysis

Fuzzy-based community detection, trust prediction, and influence modelling in online networks.

🏥

Healthcare AI & IoT

Wearable health monitoring, ocular disease screening, and smart healthcare systems.

📊

Concept Drift & Time Series

Adaptive models for streaming data and interpretable temporal analysis for financial and IoT data.

📡

6G & UAV Systems

Ultra-reliable low-latency communication and UAV-enabled networks for next-generation connectivity.
