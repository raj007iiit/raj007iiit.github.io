A Fuzzy ART Inspired Overlapping Community Detection Method for Online Social Networks
Raj E.D., Babu L.D.D.
Knowledge-Based Systems, Vol. 113, 2016, pp. 75-87 [IF: 8.139 | Most Cited]

Solves the stability-plasticity dilemma using a Fuzzy ART-inspired two-stage algorithm for overlapping community detection in OSNs. Network measures including betweenness centrality and edge betweenness drive community assignment.

📄 Scholar
Flocking Based Evolutionary Computation Strategy for Measuring Centrality of Online Social Networks
Dhinesh Babu L.D., Raj E.D.
Applied Soft Computing, Vol. 58, 2017, pp. 495-516 [IF: 8.263 | Highest IF]

FBCS models network nodes as boid agents governed by cohesion, separation, and alignment rules. Outperforms degree, betweenness, closeness, eigenvector, and PageRank centrality with superior robustness.

📄 Scholar
Information Granulation-Based Community Detection for Social Networks
Raj E.D., Manogaran G., Srivastava G., Wu Y.
IEEE Trans. Computational Social Systems, Vol. 8, 2021, pp. 122-133 [IF: 4.747 | IEEE TCSS]

GBCD uses rough-set granular computing and Object Community Factors (OCF) to assign community membership. Achieves cumulative score 3.99, beating Louvain, Girvan-Newman, LPA and CNM.

📄 Scholar
Incentivized Federated Learning: A Survey
Nair A.K., Coleri S., Sahoo J., Cenkeramaddi L.R., Raj E.D.
IEEE Trans. Emerging Topics in Computational Intelligence, Vol. 9, 2025, pp. 3190-3209 [SCI | IEEE Survey 2025]

Taxonomy of FL incentive mechanisms: Auction Theory (Forward, Reverse, Combinatorial), Contract Theory, Game Theory (Stackelberg, Non-cooperative, Coalitional), Shapley Value, and Reputation-based mechanisms.

📄 Scholar
Privacy Preserving Federated Learning Framework for IoMT Based Big Data Analysis using Edge Computing
Nair A.K., Sahoo J., Raj E.D.
Computer Standards and Interfaces, Vol. 86, 2023 [IF: 3.6 | FL + IoMT]

Multi-tier FL combining partial gradient transfer, Laplacian differential privacy, and edge computing for secure IoMT big data analysis without centralising sensitive health data.

📄 Scholar
Improved Image Dehazing Model with Color Correction Transform-Based Dark Channel Prior
Thomas J., Raj E.D.
The Visual Computer, Vol. 40, No. 12, 2024, pp. 8767-8790 [SCI | Springer Visual Computer 2024]

CCTDCP introduces White Balance Color Correction Transform into the dark channel prior pipeline with gamma correction and guided-filter refinement. Achieves superior PSNR/SSIM with no blue-cast artefacts.

📄 Scholar
FYEO: A Character Level Model for Lip Reading
Joshi V.S., Raj E.D.
IEEE ICSCC 2021 - International Conference on Smart Computing and Communications [IEEE ICSCC 2021]

End-to-end lip-reading: OpenCV+DLib preprocessing, 3D-CNN Encoder, Time-Distributed Flatten, BiGRU Decoder, CTC layer. Achieves 72.8% CER on GRID corpus.

📄 Scholar
Efficiency and Performance Optimization in LLMs through Information Bottleneck Fine-Tuning
Jo A.A., Raj E.D., Sahoo J.
ACM Transactions on Intelligent Systems and Technology, Vol. 16, 2025 [SCI | ACM TIST 2025]

4-stage IB fine-tuning: Model Selection, IB Objective (minimise I(X;Z), maximise I(Z;Y)), Hyperparameter Optimisation (Beta, batch size), Evaluation vs LoRA via attention visualisation and sensitivity analysis.
